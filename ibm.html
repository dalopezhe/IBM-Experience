Highlights

My Account

I joined the IBM Woodside Team as a design intern, working closely with Fabian Faes (Design Director), Aryana Eraman (Designer and my internship buddy), and Graham Twiggs, my lead on a UX improvement project for an existing application. They were my go-to team, always there to answer questions and support me through each step.

Contributions to Future Lab

I had the opportunity to join the Future Lab Marketing and Events Team and assist in organizing Future Lab events, as well as contribute to the inaugural editions of the "LabBuzz Newsletter." It was an incredible experience to collaborate with the team, participate in weekly meetings, and share my ideas.

Personal Reflection

Reflecting on my internship, I feel grateful for the immense learning and growth I experienced. The journey challenged me in new ways, from collaborating with talented teams to developing design solutions and navigating complex tools. My projects allowed me to deepen my skills while fostering adaptability, creativity, and clear communication. Working with supportive mentors and in such an engaging environment was invaluable, offering insights and confidence that have enriched both my personal and professional life. This experience has genuinely shaped my career path, and I’m excited for what lies ahead.
